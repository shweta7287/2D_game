Rules

  • Press "Space" to kill enemies.
  • Use key Up and Key Down to move Player.
  • Grasp "Lucky Fish"to increase stamina and gun power.
  • If you kill the "Lucky Fish" then it decrease the score by 1.
  • Score will increase after killing enemies otherwise you lost stamina.
  • Game will end if stamina is 0 OR your score is less then predefined score.
